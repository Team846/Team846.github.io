About Our Website

Custom Content Management System Built from Scratch

In order to provide our members with a unified interface to perform tasks on the website, such as posting updates, signing up for events, and placing orders, we built our own content management system from scratch using PHP and MySQL. It includes a fully featured post and page system, an event and carpool management system, an order management system, and a media gallery system, all integrated into one dashboard. Every single feature, including searching, RSS/Atom feeds, and the carousel, was coded from scratch without the use of external code. This enables us to add any features we need with much flexibility.


Custom Coded Design

Our members designed each of our pages with CSS and HTML 5, ensuring that each page looks brilliant. We coded a custom theme from scratch using the theme system from our CMS. We focused on making every element on every page match our theme, including creating our own YouTube video player using Adobe Flash Professional.


Member-contributed Content

Our updates and page content are written by our own members. Using our post system on our website, we make sure that members post new updates regularly whenever exciting events take place. As a result, our website is always up to date with new articles around every week.


Computer-designed Artwork

The talented artists on our team computer designed each individual graphic on this website using Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, and Adobe Flash Professional, making sure that our design style is maintained.
