Safety

Maintaining a safety procedure is very important to ensure the safety of each participating member during build season.  A good procedure includes making sure members know all the rules, having a checklist for approved members on each machine, as well as verifying each member's medical conditions.

To do this, Lynbrook Robotics has created a safety system on our website where we have all members read over and sign the rules of our workshop, as well as store their medical information.  We have also created a safety manual with rules and guidelines about each machine.  Our safety manual also includes a checklist of approved members for each machine.
