Finance

Having adequate financial resources is very important to every team. Every year, Lynbrook Robotics writes grants to corporate sponsors for their financial support.

Things to keep in mind when writing grants

  • Keep the style professional
  • Make an outline first
  • Keep track of deadlines
  • Verify that the criteria that the company has specified have been met
  • Refer to points from previous grants
